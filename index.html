Who?

Contrary to the image above, I currently live in the rather flat (but lake-filled) Minneapolis, MN. I am a web developer, linguist and data nerd. I’ve been in print, photographed news, and typeset the news, but mostly now I just work with the web, creating tools for a variety of goals: human language, political stuff, and my own entertainment.

In my free time, I travel, wander the cities, play a little Irish music, teach myself new languages and dialects of my own, and occupy myself with local politics.
