Minneapolis CPED Business Licenses & Consumer Services - maintains following business license types relating to parking. Could reasonably do a data request for all of these License Application forms (http://www.ci.minneapolis.mn.us/www/groups/public/@regservices/documents/webcontent/convert_270897.pdf) and then count the number in 'actual number of vehicle parking spaces'. This is doable, however numbers may be fudged by license requesters.
