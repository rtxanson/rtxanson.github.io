Hello, again

2013-09-12

tl;dr: Been around, now seeking opportunities to write.

It’s been a while since I’ve written on the web at any length. Most of the problem has been actually sitting down and writing, but another significant chunk of the issue has been where to write. My old blog was just too cludgy to deal with. It was one of my first programming projects, and helped me learn some things, but obviously if the goal was to just write, well, I’d not have rolled my own thing.

This time around, I’m using a static site generator, hyde, which gives me a bigger amount of flexibility, and has so far it’s been easy to import old content, too, but I might keep that to a minimum. Anyway, I’m raising my glass (and attempting not to struggle with it) to a future of some more fun with writing.
